Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?

Earned money is stored in your WatchTox wallet. Once you reach the $10 minimum you can request a PayPal or bank transfer.

Can I watch any YouTube video?

Only videos that have been approved by advertisers and verified as owned by them are available in our queue.

Is there a limit on how many videos I can watch per day?

We recommend up to 100 videos per day to keep the system fair. Excessive activity may trigger a throttling check.

How are lottery winners selected?

Winners are picked using a cryptographically secure random number generator (Node.js Crypto). All draws are logged for transparency.
